Talkfeed is a tool to receive new posts from RSS and Atom feeds directly in your GTalk /Jabber client.



Free tool, no inscription required.

The application is hosted on Google App Engine : http://talkfeed.appspot.com



![http://code.google.com/appengine/images/appengine-silver-120x30.gif](http://code.google.com/appengine/images/appengine-silver-120x30.gif)



&lt;hr/&gt;



# Get started #

  * Add the **"talkfeed (arobase) appspot.com"** address to your contacts in GTalk / Jabber.
  * Enter websites urls in the chat interface.
  * When new posts are published, the TalkFeed bot send you an alert !
